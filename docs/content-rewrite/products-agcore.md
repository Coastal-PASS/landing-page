# AgSupport Platform (Beta)  
### A unified support and fleet management portal for agriculture

AgSupport is our upcoming SaaS platform designed for growers and dealerships who want clearer visibility and more efficient support workflows.

## Key Features (Planned)
- Work order management  
- Fleet tracking  
- RTK access management  
- Customer communication tools  
- Seasonal maintenance scheduling  
- Dealership multi-tenant support mode  

## Status
Under active development. Early partners may receive pre-release access.

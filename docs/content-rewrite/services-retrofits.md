# Sprayer Retrofits (Raven ARA & DirectSteer)  
### Run modern control systems on your existing sprayers

We retrofit older sprayers with modern controls including Raven ARA (Advanced Rate Automation) and DirectSteer for improved accuracy and reduced drift.

## What we install
- Raven ARA systems  
- DirectSteer steering retrofits  
- Boom control modules  
- Section and rate controllers  
- Custom cab harnessing and hardware integration  

## Why it matters
- Upgrade without replacing equipment  
- Reduce chemical waste  
- Improve vineyard and specialty crop accuracy  
- Extend the lifespan of existing sprayers
